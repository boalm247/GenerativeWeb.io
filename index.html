week 4

The Jodi reading discussed how designers view their work in regards to a digital platform; whether they see themselves as working 'on' or 'within' the digital. My initial raction is that I work 'within' the digital. I percieve space quite differently on a computer than real life, although they are not mutually exclusive. The only time I am consciously aware of the interaction between digital and real is when I watch someone else interact with it. When I myself use digital platforms I feel transported to another world.
